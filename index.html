Core Technology

Three problems. One protocol.

Storage networks can't verify compute. Compute networks can't prove storage. Neither can route traffic. Prova does all three with math, not trust.

Compute

Quantized Bisection Proofs

INT8 quantized inference is deterministic. When nodes disagree, binary search finds the diverging layer in O(log L) steps. Verify one layer, not the whole model. Under 2% compute cost.

Storage

Three-Tier Proof System

PDP for fast onboarding (minutes), TEE-attested for instant storage (seconds), optional PoRep for cold archival. All tiers earn equally. Math is the foundation, hardware is the optimization.

Networking

Chain-Managed Connectivity

Real IPv6 addresses for every VM. HTTP/HTTPS over IPv4 via SNI routing. Bandwidth metering on-chain. DDoS protection per-VM. Your server is as reachable as one on AWS.

Economics

1 Byte = 1 Byte

No multipliers. No privileged data. Every byte earns equally. Three reward streams split by demand across storage, compute, and network. 50% fee burn post-halving.

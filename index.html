Core Technology

Two problems. One protocol.

Storage networks can't verify compute. Compute networks can't prove storage. Prova does both, with math, not trust.

Compute Verification

Quantized Bisection Proofs

INT8 quantized neural networks produce deterministic outputs. When two nodes disagree, binary search finds the diverging layer in O(log L) steps. Verify one layer instead of re-running the entire model. Less than 2% of original compute cost.

Storage — Baseline

Provable Data Possession

Lightweight Merkle proofs over raw data. No sealing, no hours-long onboarding. Data stored in minutes, proven continuously. Logarithmic gas scaling from 100 to 10,000 roots.

Storage — Fast Path

TEE-Attested Proofs

Hardware-managed encryption with unique replicas per machine. Onboarding in seconds. Open-source enclave, chain-versioned, governance-approved. Falls back to PDP if TEE is compromised. Hardware accelerates, never replaces math.

Economics

One Byte = One Byte

No quality multipliers. No privileged data classes. No DataCap. Every byte earns equally. Dual reward streams split dynamically between storage and compute based on network demand.
